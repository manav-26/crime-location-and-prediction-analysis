Why is it needed?

Trends of crimes in India keep changing with the growing population and rapid development of towns and cities. To curb crime, the Police need countless hours to go through the crime data. There is a necessity for tools that can help us analyze and predict crime rate in order to ensure proper surveillance. Common people can also understand the crime rate and trends without going through the large clusters of data and jargons. By spotting the crime prone areas the general public can be given an alert about the crimes in different parts of a country.The data used for our system provides all necessary information about crimes, required for our analysis. Using this data, we will be able to provide inferences and draw decisions on the severity and location of the crime.

Who will be benifitted?

  • It can help us to analyze and perform comparative study in crime rate and infer various useful conclusions.
  • The prediction model will help to get a better idea to possible crime rate in near future.
  • By spotting the crime prone areas the general public can be given an alert about the crimes in different parts of a country.
  • It can help the concerned authorities to analyze and predict crime rate in order to ensure proper surveillance.
  • The data set developed by us over a period of time through web scraping can be further used for research.
 

Which technologies are used ?

To develop this project, we have used Python, Flask framework, web scraping and crawling, zing charts, Beautiful soup library, pandas, FB Prophet library for implementing maps and Machine Learning.
